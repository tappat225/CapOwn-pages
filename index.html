Why it exists

Give your Agent remote hands without turning every machine into an Agent workstation.

CapOwn keeps the full AI Agent setup on your trusted client machine. Lightweight Workers connect outward to a Master relay, then execute bounded file, shell, and system tasks on the devices you already own.

01 Client

Your local Agent calls CapOwn through CLI, Skill, SDK, or adapter.

02 Master

The relay handles identity, routing, ownership checks, and task state.

03 Worker

Remote devices maintain outbound connections and execute inside configured bounds.
